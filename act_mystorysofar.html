My Story So Far Guide

Life Visioning Tool

Activity Guide and Scripting

I would love to run through an activity with you to explore your life’s journey to date, where you want to be headed and what is most important to you. It’s called “My story”.

Complete form with client age and details as required and click on start activity button.

What are top 5 key milestone moments in the story of your life so far? What are the most important or significant milestone events or achievements that have impacted your quality of life? Are there any on this list that apply? Lets drop that in at the right age on the top green panel. We can add in customised event if you like. (Growth life event list) > Likely to offer the usual suspects so after establishing 5 if easy can probe a little deeper for more events if appropriate.

Every good story has some twists and turns, some ups and downs. What are some of the biggest milestone challenges you have faced in your life story so far? (Challenging life event list) Lets drop that in at the right age on the bottom red panel.

Scale on Y axis relative to impact on their quality of life. Probe how they coped with the challenges. What worked for them best?

Click on Next chapter and show future timeline and refocus on this section i.e. now until ‘the end’

Ok so we have our back story. Lets look at writing the sequel! What happens next? Which are the key aspirational milestone events or goals you are looking forward to in the future? (Future Goal life event list).

Which are the key milestone events you may face in the future that you will find more challenging? (Future Obstacle life event list) .

Scale on Y axis relative to impact on their quality of life.

Click on Future Confidence and refocus on these ratings.

How confident do you currently feel in being able to achieve these key goals you have identified? Work though each. How confident do you currently feel in being able to navigate these key obstacles you have identified?

Adjust vertical sliders with scale on Y axis relative to confidence to achieve goals and deal with challenges. Probe why they feel this way.

(If judged appropriate)

Ultimately what does the happy ending to your life story read like? (Add goals as required. Capture the epitaph? Consider leaving conversation with lighter tone by asking “What would the biopic of your life be called? Who would you get to play you?)
