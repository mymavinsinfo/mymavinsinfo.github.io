Quality of Life Tracking

Overview

Why Quality of Life Matters

Adopting a whole person and whole of life approach has been shown to increase client engagement and retention as well as overall planning effectiveness (refer to reference resources The Good Life).

Quality of life is the ‘ends to the means’ of financial planning. An adviser’s ability to help clients adjust the trajectory towards this is at the heart of the value they provide.

It provides an ongoing gauge of the effectiveness of the advice relationship that can be experienced as a feeling and intuitively understood.

It gets to the heart of prioritising client’s best interests (and those of advisers).

Quality of Life and Best Interests

Quality of Life Drivers

Our approach to gauging Quality of Life avoids preconceptions and abstraction of wellbeing and happiness constructs by focusing on the key proven drivers of quality of life that an adviser can help move the dial for clients and make a demonstrable impact.

This also provides a diagnostic tool to build self-awareness and structure meaningful interactions with clients that earn the right to understand their deeper personal needs and experience in life.

This is a powerful benchmark to anchor plans and value provided over the relationship journey.
